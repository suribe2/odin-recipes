Lemon Bars

Home

Description

Lemon bar recepi featuring a rich and flavorfull filling and a crumbly cookie crust.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Lemons

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Prehead oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Blen to cups flour, butter and 1/2 cup sugar in a medium bowl until well combined.
  4. Press crust into the bottom of an ungreased 9x13-inch pan.
  5. Bake crust in the preheated oven until firm and golden, about 15 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, whisk 1/2 cups sugar and 1/4 cup flour in a medium bowl, and whisk in eggs and lemon juice until smooth to make the filling.
  7. Pour filling over the baked crust.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 20 min. Set pan aside to let the bars cool down and get firm.
  9. When cooled, cutthe bars into squares.
