Ice Cream Cake

Home

Description

The sweetest way to celebrate an occasion. This ice cream cake is shockingly easy to make with just five ingredients. A nostaligic treat for many, many of us have enjoyed this at a birthday somewhere. The perfect combination of texture: rich, dense cake with cold, sweet ice cream. Some would call it perfect.

Ingredients

  • Cake Mix
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Ice Cream

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Combine cake mix, water, eggs, and oil in a large bowl. Beat with electric mixer until smooth. Pour batter into baking dish.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick incerted in the center comes out clean, about 26 to 30 minutes. Allow to cool completely on wired rack.
  4. Remove carton paper from ice cram, set ice cream block onto long side. Use piece of string to cut in half lengthwise then place the 2 pieces side by side on a sheet of waxed paper.
  5. Remove cooled cake from baking dish and place ovr ice cream slabs. Trim cake and ice cream until edges are flush.
  6. Place aboard over the cake. while holding waxed paper, flip ice cream cake over. Remove waxed paper and smooth the seam between ice cream slabs.
  7. Cover with waxed paper and freeze until very firm. Decorate as desired.
